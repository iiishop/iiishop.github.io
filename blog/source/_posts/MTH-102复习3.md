# Lec7
1. ## Continuous random variables
	1. ### Probability density function (pdf)
		Let $X$ be a random variable over the outcome space $S$ which is an interval or union of intervals. $X$ is called a **continuous random variable** if there exists an integrable function $f(x)$ satisfying the following:
		***(a)*** $f(x)\geq 0,\ x\in \mathbb{R}$
		***(b)*** $\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(x)dx=1$ 
		***(c)*** $if\ a\leq b,\ then$$$P(a\leq X\leq b)=\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx$$
		$f(x)$ is called the **Probability density function (pdf)** of $X$
	2. ### Character
		对于一个连续随机变量X，它在实数域上任意一特定点的概率都是0$$P(X=a)=0\ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ a\in\mathbb{R}$$
		因此$$P(X\leq a)=P(X<a\cup X=a)=P(X<a)+P(X=a)=P(X<a)$$$$P(a\leq X\leq b)=P(a<X\leq b)=P(a\leq X<b)=P(a<X<b)$$
	3. ### Cumulative distribution function (cdf)
		The **Cumulative distribution function (cdf)** of a continuous random variable $X$ with the pdf $f(x)$ is given by $$F(x)=P(X\leq x)=\int_{-\infty}^{x}f(t)dt\ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ x\in\mathbb{R}$$Although $P(X\leq x)=P(X<x)$, we write $P(X\leq x)$ for consistency with the cdf of discrete random variables.
		- #### Properties
			- $F(x)$ is continuous, and for $x$ values for which the derivative $F'(x)$ exists,$$F'(x)=f(x)$$
			- For any $(a,b)\subseteq S,$ $$P(a\leq X \leq b)=F(b)-F(a)$$
1. ## Mean and variance
	1. ### Mean of the continuous random variable$$E[X]=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}xf(x)dx$$
		**Remark**. $E[X]$ max not exist
		$$E[g(X)]=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}g(x)f(x)dx$$
		2. ### Variance$$Var(X)=E[(X-E[X])^{2}]=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}(x-E[X])^{2}f(x)dx$$$$Var(X)=E[X^{2}]-(E[X])^{2}=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}x^{2}f(x)dx-(E[X])^{2}$$
		- #### Properties
			- The variance is always nonnegative
			- The square root of $Var(X)$, i.e. $\sqrt{Var(X)}$, is called the standard deviation of $X$
			- Let $X$ be a random variable, $a,b$, and $c$ be constants. Consider $Y=aX+b$ as a linear function of $X$. Then
				- $$Var(c)=0$$
				- $$Var(Y)=Var(aX+b)=a^{2}Var(X)$$
1. ## Uniform distribution
	A random variable $X$ is said to be uniformly distributed over the interval $(a,b)$ if its probability density function is given by$$f(x)=\left\{\begin{array}{lc}\frac{1}{b-a}&a<x<b\\0&otherwise\end{array}\right.$$The cdf of $X$ is $$F(X)=\left\{\begin{array}{lc}0&x\leq a\\\frac{x-a}{b-a}&a<x<b\\1&x\geq b\end{array}\right.$$The mean and variance of $X$ are $$E(X)=\frac{a+b}{2}$$$$Var(X)=\frac{(b-a)^{2}}{12}$$

# Lec8
1. ## Exponential distribution
	- ### Definition
		If a continuous random variable $X$ is said to be an exponential random variable. The pdf is $$f(x)=\left\{\begin{array}{lc}\lambda e^{-\lambda x}&x\geq 0\\0&x<0\end{array}\right.$$
		The cdf of $X$ is$$F(x)=P(X\leq x)=\int_{-\infty}^{x}f(t)dt=\left\{\begin{array}{lc}1-e^{-\lambda x}&x\geq 0\\0&x<0\end{array}\right.$$
		The mean and variance are$$E(X)=\frac{1}{\lambda}$$$$Var(X)=\frac{1}{\lambda^{2}}$$
	- ### Applications
		指数分布通常应用于预测直到某个特定事件发生前的时间长度。
		泊松分布通常应用于预测在某个特定的时间间隔中事件发生的次数。
		事实上，泊松分布事件发生的间隔时间是服从指数分布的，如果$\lambda_{1}$和$\lambda_{2}$是泊松分布的参数和相关指数分布的参数，那么$\lambda_{1}=\lambda_{2}$
	- ### Memoryless property
		指数分布是无记忆的，即当$t,s>0时$$$P(X>t+s|X>s)=P(X>t)$$
		证明：因为是指数分布，所以$P(X\leq t)=1-e^{-\lambda t}\ \ for\ t\geq 0$因此$$P(X>t)=e^{-\lambda t}$$如此可得$$P(X>t+s|X>s)=\frac{e^{-\lambda(t+s)}}{e^{-\lambda s}}=e^{-\lambda t}=P(X>t)$$
		当 $X$ 是等待一辆公交车的时间，并且是遵循指数分布，拥有参数$\lambda$的连续随机变量。
		乘客A比乘客B更早到达公交车站，当乘客B到达车站时，乘客A已经等待了s分钟，并且车还没来。
		已知乘客A已经等待了s分钟，那么他再等t分钟的概率是$P(X>t+s|X>s)$，乘客B再等t分钟的概率是$P(X>t)$因为指数分布的无记忆，他们会再等待t分钟的概率相等，同时这也符合现实规律。
1. ## Normal distribution
	- ### Definition
		当一个随机变量$X$ 符合正态分布，其pdf是$$f(x)=\frac{1}{\sigma\sqrt{2\pi}}\exp[-\frac{(x-\mu)^{2}}{2\sigma^{2}}],\ \ for\ \ x\in\mathbb{R}$$
		当$\mu,\sigma\in\mathbb{R}$是阐述，我们可以说$X\ is\ N(\mu,\sigma^{2})$
		那么$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\exp[-\frac{x^{2}}{2}]dx=\sqrt{2\pi}$$
		令$z=\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}$我们可以得到$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}f(x)dx=\int_{-\infty}^{\infty}\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}}\exp[-\frac{z^{2}}{2}]dz=1$$
		X的期望和方差分别是$$E(X)=\mu$$$$Var(X)=\sigma ^{2}$$
	- ### Standard normal distribution
		当$Z\ is\ N(0,1)$我们称Z遵循标准正态分布，这时，Z的cdf时$$\Phi(z)=P(Z\leq z)=\int_{-\infty}^{z} \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}}e^{\frac{-w^{2}}{2}dw}, \ \ for\ \ z\in\mathbb{R}$$
		$但z\geq0时\Phi(z)的值会如下表所示$![标准正态分布.png](标准正态分布.png)
		因为当任意实数z存在时$$\Phi(-z)=1-\Phi(z)$$
		由此可得: $\Phi(0)=0.5$
	- ### Normal distribution theorem
		1. 当X遵循正态分布$N(\mu,\sigma^{2})$那么$Z=\frac{X-\mu}{\sigma}=N(0,1)$
		2. 当X遵循正态分布$N(\mu,\sigma^{2})$那么对于任何$[a,b]\subseteq\mathbb{R}$$$P(a\leq X\leq b)=P(\frac{a-\mu}{\sigma}\leq\frac{X-\mu}{\sigma}\leq\frac{b-\mu}{\sigma})=\Phi(\frac{b-\mu}{\sigma})-\Phi(\frac{a-\mu}{\sigma})$$
		3. 当对于任何$c>0$$$P(|X-\mu|<c\sigma)=P(\mu-c\sigma\leq X\leq\mu+c\sigma)=2\Phi(c)-1$$
1. ## Rayleigh distribution
	- ### Definition
		瑞丽分布的动机是测量射击距离与射点到靶心的距离。$$R=\sqrt{X^{2}+Y^{2}}$$当$X,Y$是射点的坐标，并且服从正态分布$N(0,\sigma^{2}),那么R$拥有参数为$\sigma$的瑞丽分布
	- ### Rayleigh distribution
		- The pdf$$f(r)=\frac{r}{\sigma^{2}}e^{-\frac{r^{2}}{2\sigma^{2}}},\ \ for\ \ r>0$$
		- The cdf$$F(r)=P(R\leq r)=1-e^{-\frac{r^{2}}{2\sigma^{2}}},\ \ for\ \ r>0$$
		- The mean and variance$$E(R)=\sigma\sqrt{\frac{\pi}{2}}$$$$Var(R)=\frac{4-\pi}{2}\sigma^{2}$$
